Benefits

Instead of fighting technology, tgoma leverages technology to transform “screen time” from an isolating, sedentary activity, into a healthful, engaging experience for the entire family.

tgoma’s games and apps are designed to be fun for both kids and adults, offering an effective physical workout. The tgoma games integrated with a Springfree Trampoline challenge the entire family while increasing their time outside in the backyard.
